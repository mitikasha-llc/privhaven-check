Verify a PrivHaven compliance report

Everything runs in your browser. Your report.json and your original file never leave this page — no upload, and no PrivHaven engine. The check uses only public algorithms (checksums + SHA-256).

    This tool is a convenience. Its source is public and hash-pinned, and it runs offline — so you never have to trust the copy you loaded. Verify what a PrivHaven report claims; a compliance conclusion is still your auditor's to draw.
